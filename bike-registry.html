Annex 1. You and your bicycle

Information and rules about you and your bicycle.

Legal UK Bicycle Registration

Your bike should be properly registered as soon as possible immediately after purchase.

The registration procedure includes the following steps:

  1. Notifying as many friends as possible about the purchase. This can be done either through social networks, or by writing directly to everyone.
  2. Send or publish a photo of the bike with the caption "My handsome".
  3. Sign up with Strava and post your first short ride around the courtyards (no more than 1 - 2 miles).

There can be no good reason why you should not register your bike. Even if you rode it 3 times a year.

After that, your bike can be considered registered and admitted to public roads.
